JarInspector Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Overview

JarInspector respects your privacy. This policy explains how JarInspector handles information when you use the app.

Data Collection

JarInspector does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data. The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party crash reporting SDKs.

Local File Processing

JarInspector lets you open JAR, ZIP, and Java class files. File contents, manifest data, archive entries, class metadata, dependency context, and decompiled source previews are processed locally on your device. The app does not upload your files or file contents to Coobbi servers.

Local Storage

JarInspector may store local copies of opened files, recent file references, generated Java source previews, diagnostics reports, and inspection reports on your device so that you can reopen, inspect, export, or share them. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to Coobbi.

Network and External Links

JarInspector does not use network connections to collect user data. The app may open external links when you tap support, contact, or privacy policy links.

The decompiler runtime is bundled with the app and runs locally. It is not used by Coobbi for advertising, analytics, or tracking.

Payments

If JarInspector is purchased through the App Store, payment processing is handled by Apple. Coobbi does not receive your payment card details.

Children's Privacy

JarInspector does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Components

JarInspector includes bundled runtime components used to inspect and decompile Java class files locally. These components are included to provide app functionality and are not used by Coobbi for advertising, analytics, or tracking.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at imzine.com@gmail.com.
