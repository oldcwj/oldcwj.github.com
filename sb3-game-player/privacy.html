SB3 Game Player Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Overview

SB3 Game Player respects your privacy. This policy explains how SB3 Game Player handles information when you use the app.

Data Collection

SB3 Game Player does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data. The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party crash reporting SDKs.

Local File Processing

The app lets you open and play SB3 project files. SB3 files, project contents, assets, sounds, and project.json data are processed locally on your device. The app does not upload your SB3 files or project contents to Coobbi servers.

Local Storage

SB3 Game Player may store local copies of opened SB3 files and a recent projects list on your device so that you can reopen projects more easily. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to Coobbi.

Network and External Links

SB3 Game Player does not use network connections to collect user data. The app may open external links when you tap support, contact, or privacy policy links.

The app includes a local player runtime for SB3 projects. If a user-provided SB3 project or extension attempts to access external resources, those requests are controlled by that project or runtime behavior and are not used by Coobbi to collect personal data.

Children's Privacy

SB3 Game Player does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Components

SB3 Game Player includes open-source components used to load and play SB3 projects locally. These components are included to provide playback functionality and are not used by Coobbi for advertising, analytics, or tracking.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at imzine.com@gmail.com.
