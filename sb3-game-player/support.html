SB3 Game Player Support

Help and contact information for SB3 Game Player on iPhone and iPad.

About SB3 Game Player

SB3 Game Player is an iOS app for opening and playing SB3 project files. The app is designed for local project playback on your device.

SB3 Game Player is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the Scratch Foundation or MIT.

Contact Support

If you need help, want to report a bug, or have a feature request, contact us by email.

Email: imzine.com@gmail.com

When reporting an issue, please include the app version, your iPhone or iPad model, iOS version, a short description of the issue, and steps to reproduce it.

Before Sending Files

SB3 files are processed locally by the app. Please do not send private or sensitive SB3 project files unless they are necessary for troubleshooting and you are comfortable sharing them.
